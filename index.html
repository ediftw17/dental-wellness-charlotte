Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Sergei Shirman, DDS

With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Shirman founded Dental Wellness of Charlotte on a simple belief: every patient deserves thoughtful, unhurried care in a comfortable environment.

His patient-first philosophy emphasizes listening, education, and shared decision-making. Dr. Shirman combines advanced training in implants, cosmetic dentistry, and digital imaging with a gentle chairside manner that puts even anxious patients at ease.

Through ongoing continuing education and investment in modern techniques and technology, Dr. Shirman ensures every patient receives the highest standard of care available today.
